Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How does this work?

nyx.rs is a relay service. You select a service, provide a link, and we place an order on your behalf with our upstream provider. We act as a privacy-preserving layer between you and the service provider.

Q: Do I need an account?

No. This service is completely account-free.

Q: What is an Order ID?

After you place an order, you will receive a unique Order ID (a UUID). This is your only proof of purchase. You MUST save it. You can use this ID on the homepage to check the status of your order at any time.

Q: Is it safe to use?

We are designed with privacy as the top priority. We don't log your IP, we don't require personal info, and our code is open source so you can verify our claims. However, as with any new service, you should proceed with caution.

Q: What if my order fails or I need help?

Please contact us at the email address listed in the footer of the homepage. Provide your Order ID so we can investigate the issue with our provider.
